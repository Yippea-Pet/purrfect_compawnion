# Purrfect Compawnion
A mobile application that accompanies you throughout your journey.
Available on Android

## Aim
We aim to create a mobile Android pet application with a pet (based on the mascot of NUS School of Computing(SoC), Soccat) that you can take care of and will accompany you. It can provide companionship to users by allowing users to interact with their pets, and also boost usersâ€™ productivity by rewarding them for completing tasks.
